BACKGROUND INFORMATION

This foundation started on 11th June 2023 by JOSHUA BRIGHT EJOK. He was moved by the sufferings that the street children were going through and growing up from a similar background, he knew thier pain and decided to do something about it. He knew that if we are all children of God, then we all deserve the best life on earth.

VISION

"Promoting the rights and well being of every child in everything we do."

MISSION

"Equipping children with life skills development to transition them into adulthood and to become leaders in their communities."

OBJECTIVES OF THIS FOUNDATION.

  • To empower orphans and the less priviledged in society through vocational training and self reliant programs.
  • To put smiles on the faces of the street children by providing palliatives in form of food stuffs.
  • To encourage and educate the populance on the importance of supporting charitable organisations.
